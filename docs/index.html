About the Project

The Material Forecasting System was developed to help businesses optimize their inventory management by providing accurate forecasts of future material needs.

By analyzing historical consumption patterns and accounting for existing stock levels, the system helps reduce carrying costs, prevent stockouts, and improve overall supply chain efficiency.

The application is built with Python, Flask, Pandas, Statsmodels, and Plotly, providing a powerful yet user-friendly interface for material forecasting.
