Material Forecasting for Financial Year

This application is specifically designed to forecast material needs for the next financial year (April 1st to March 31st). The forecast takes into account:

  • Historical consumption patterns
  • Current stock levels (subtracted from consumption forecast)
  • Pending deliveries (if provided)
Material Forecast vs. Consumption Forecast:
Consumption Forecast: Predicts how much will be consumed based on historical patterns
Material Forecast: Predicts how much new material you need to order, after accounting for existing stock
